Bottoms Up Tiki Tours

Bottom's Up is Chincoteague Island's most unique and intimate boat charter. Let your worries melt away as our tiki captain and crew whisk you away from bustling city life for an unforgettable adventure. Our 40' tiki charter boat can accommodate a variety of group sizes and events. We have a cruise for everybody. Whatever your cruise desire we can make it happen.

Bites and Bottles

You can bring your own drinks and snacks or let us take care of the food details with our catering options or try one of our special catered and themed cruises.

Safe and Secure

At Bottom's Up, our guests' comfort, safety, and satisfaction are our top priority. All our charters include a Coast Guard licensed captain, approved safety devices, and a bathroom on-board.

Tiki Vibes

Our boat features an amazing sound system, and our DJ curated playlists will quickly have you jammin'. You can also hook into our sound system and DJ your own party.

Time With Your Tribe

Bottom's Up Tiki Tours are the perfect way to celebrate any occasion! Birthdays, proposals, wedding parties, girls/guys night out, graduation parties, team building, corporate meetings, retirement, bachelorette/bachelor, anniversary parties, or any reason you can think of... or for no reason at all.

How Do You Tiki?

We have a multitude of different cruises and adventures to match your vibe. All cruises can be booked as a private charter if you prefer. Give us a call to create your personal specialty cruise.

Cancellation Policy

Customers must cancel within 7 days of the scheduled tour. Any day after the 7 day time period has passed, you will receive a voucher for a future tour. Customers will also receive a voucher (raincheck) for a future tour for any operator cancellations due to inclement weather conditions or any other unforeseen circumstances. No-shows will be charged full price. To cancel, contact us by phone to inquire about a cancellation.
